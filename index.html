What is Crescendo?

Crescendo: Fearfully & Wonderfully Made is a 13‑week guided journey that blends prayer, reflection, style & color exploration, and practical rhythms for daily life. It’s designed to be warm, simple, and deeply human.

  • Weekly workbook pages + guided reflections
  • Spotlight Saints & Scripture meditations
  • Gentle style/color discovery that honors dignity
  • Community support & accountability

At a glance

  • Length: 13 weeks
  • When: Tuesdays at 7:30 PM
  • Format: 60–75 min live session
  • Who: Women seeking grace & clarity

FAQ

Is this only for Catholics?

Crescendo is rooted in Catholic spirituality and warmly welcomes seekers who resonate with the approach.

What if I miss a week?

Sessions are recorded; gentle catch‑up notes are provided.

Is there homework?

Simple weekly reflections (10–20 minutes) and optional activities.
