Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 7, 2025

My Holy Crescendo

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. If you have questions, contact us at myholycrescendo@gmail.com.

1) Information We Collect

  • Information you provide — name, email address, and any messages you send us (e.g., when you subscribe to the 3‑Day Prelude or join a cohort).
  • Automatically collected data — basic, non-identifying usage data (e.g., pages viewed) if analytics are enabled. We do not collect sensitive categories.
  • Payment data — if you purchase the cohort or materials through a third party (e.g., Stripe, PayPal, Gumroad, or similar), your payment is processed by that provider. We do not store your full payment details.

2) How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver content you requested (e.g., Prelude emails, onboarding, reminders).
  • To respond to inquiries and provide support.
  • To improve the site and offerings.
  • To send occasional updates about cohorts, resources, or events (you can unsubscribe anytime).

3) Cookies & Analytics

We keep cookies to a minimum. If we enable simple analytics (e.g., privacy-friendly, cookie‑light tools), it helps us understand which pages are most helpful. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

4) Sharing & Third Parties

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may use trusted providers (email delivery, payments, signup forms). They only process your data to provide their service to us and must protect it.
  • Links to other sites (e.g., Spotify, community platforms) are governed by those sites’ policies.

5) Email Choices

You can unsubscribe from non‑essential emails at any time using the link in the email or by contacting us at myholycrescendo@gmail.com.

6) Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary for the purposes described here, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7) Children’s Privacy

Our site and programs are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8) Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your information. No online transmission or storage system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

9) Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your information. To make a request, email myholycrescendo@gmail.com.

10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We’ll change the “Effective date” above when we do. Material changes will be communicated on this page.

11) Contact

If you have questions about this policy or our practices, contact us at myholycrescendo@gmail.com.
