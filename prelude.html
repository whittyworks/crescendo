Inside the Prelude

Three days, three movements

Day 1 — Tune In

Quiet the noise, breathe, and notice. Reflect with Psalm 139:1‑6, 13‑14 — you are known, seen, and wonderfully made.

Day 2 — Gather the Notes

Name what is beautiful and what is burdened. A simple gratitude + naming practice to bring heart and mind into harmony.

Day 3 — Create a Rest

Set one small, sustainable rhythm of rest. Learn a 5‑minute pause you can keep using after the Prelude ends.
